Reading

Here is a list of my favourite series!

Ruby Redfort - Lauren Child

Ruby is a teenage genius who is recruited by a secret spy agency to crack codes for them. This book series for younger teenage readers is made by Ruby’s wonderful sense of humour.

Here is the website

Chronicles of Ixia - Maria V Snyder

About to be executed as a criminal, Yelena is offered a choice, die or become the Commander’s food taster. Despite the restraints placed on her, Yelena revels in her newfound freedom. But it will not last long as she is dragged into the troubles of her realm.

Here is the website

The Black Magician - Trudi Canavan

When Sonea, a slum dweller, spontaneously develops magical talents which are supposed to be restricted to the upper class, she is hunted by the magician’s guild. But she distrusts them and flees, as her magic spirals out of control.

Here is the website

Heralds of Valdemar - Mercedes Lackey

Chosen by a spirit horse to be a Herald of Valdemar - messenger, spy, protector and magician - Talia, a girl from a simple steadholt background, is also expected to be the queen’s companion and advisor, teach the spoilt princess to behave and thwart a plot to kill the queen and overthrow the royal family.

Here is the website

The Dresdan Files - Jim Butcher

A modern day wizard living in Chicago, Harry Dresdan advertises his skills to paying clients and also acts as a consultant to the Chicago PD Special Investigations Department. But when demons, vampires, werewolves, faeries and evil wizards spend their time trying to destroy his city and/or the world, Harry is often rather too busy to track down lost keys for his clients.
